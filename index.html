It’s been a month since we got down with the Club Tropicana spirit. Thanks for making it amazing. Here are some treasures to remind you of the good times:


video by Nate Midgley and Gumar

Just like the temperature, Club Tropicana keeps getting hotter. We were very blessed to have our buddy Suzanne Kraft (Blase/Pharaos/Odd Numbers) up from LA to join us. We were also very fortunate to have all of you beautiful people there as well.
We’ve still got some Club Tropicana Gear and it's going quick so grab it while you can over in the store section of the Bed of Roses Website.
We also got a recording of Kitchen DJ's near 5 hour DJ set. To introduce this recording is the man himself:

This is music for social creatures, for summoning that laid back feel. It was recorded live in the front room of the Tonic Lounge on May 31th, 2014 over the course of 5 or so hours. For the zoners in attendance I hope it helped them get down with their bad selves as they waited in line for a drink or chilled with their amigos.
Club Tropicana is a special night. We gotta be free, it’s true, but it’s not just flying eagles and ultimate liberty - we still gotta eat. These jams are food for your heart and your moving feet, and food is love.
-Kitchen DJ
